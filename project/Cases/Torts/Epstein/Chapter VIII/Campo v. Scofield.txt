Synopsis of Rule of Law
Under products liability a manufacturer is not responsible for obvious and known dangers of products.

Facts
No facts given.

Issue
Under products liability, is a manufacturer responsible for obvious and known dangers of products?

Held
No.

Discussion
Strict liability is not absolute liability. People are still expected to use common sense when handling a potentially dangerous product.
