Synopsis of Rule of Law
The principle behind awarding a party damages, is compensation, the equivalent in money for the actual loss caused by the wrong of another.

Facts
There are no facts in the casebook.

Issue
Is compensation the principle behind awarding a party damages?

Held
Yes.

Discussion
The purpose of an award of money damages is compensation. Compensation is given to an injured party to substitute or replace the actual loss caused by the wrongfully acts of another.
